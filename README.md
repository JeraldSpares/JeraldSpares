## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **BSIT senior** focused on shipping software that businesses actually use — not just classroom demos. My work spans **AI-powered systems**, **web platforms**, and **mobile applications**, with clients ranging from local cafés to enterprise dealerships.

- 🔭 Currently building **CafeConnect**, an online ordering platform for Hebrew's Cafe
- 🌱 Deepening my skills in **Next.js 16**, **Supabase**, and **AI agents**
- 💡 I care about clean code, sensible UX, and systems that solve real problems
- 🤝 Open to internships, freelance work, and collaboration
- 📍 Based in **Makati City, Philippines**

### 🍵 [CafeConnect — Online Ordering & Management System](https://github.com/JeraldSpares/CafeConnect-Online-Ordering-Management-System)
> A full-stack ordering and operations platform built for **Hebrew's Cafe**. Customers browse the menu, place orders, and earn loyalty rewards; staff manage inventory, orders, and analytics from a single admin dashboard.

`Next.js 16` · `Supabase` · `TypeScript` · `Tailwind CSS` · `PostgreSQL`

---

### 🚗 [DealerHub — AI-Powered Vehicle Financing](https://github.com/JeraldSpares/DealerHub-AI-Powered-Vehicle-Financing-System-Nissan-Philippines)
> An end-to-end AI system for **Nissan Philippines** that automates vehicle loan evaluations and financial processing — significantly reducing manual turnaround time.

`AI` · `HTML` · `Financial Automation`

---

### 🎓 [COPUS — PHINMA Araullo Campus System](https://github.com/JeraldSpares/COPUS-Web-and-Mobile-Campus-System-PHINMA-Araullo-University)
> A cross-platform communications and operations system that **digitized and modernized university-wide processes** at PHINMA Araullo.

`JavaScript` · `Cross-platform` · `University Systems`

---

### 🤖 [EVA Marketing AI Showcase](https://github.com/JeraldSpares/EVA-MARKETING-AI-SHOWCASE)
> A **28-agent AI marketing department** designed for Enderun Colleges — autonomous agents handling research, copywriting, design briefs, and campaign coordination.

`Python` · `Multi-Agent AI` · `Marketing Automation`

---

### 🏥 [QCAS — Quantum Care Appointment System](https://github.com/JeraldSpares/QCAS-Quantum-Care-Appointment-System)
> A user-friendly healthcare scheduling platform that lets patients and providers **book, reschedule, and cancel appointments** from any device.

`PHP` · `Healthcare` · `Scheduling`

---

### 🗺️ [LOC8 — Travel & Navigation App](https://github.com/JeraldSpares/LOC8-Travel-and-Navigation-Mobile-Application)

`Java` · `Android` · `Google Maps API`
